How to cook Scrambled Eggs

Ingredients:

  • 3 eggs
  • 2 tablespoons milk
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tablespoon butter

Instructions:

  1. Crack the eggs into a bowl.
  2. Add milk, salt, and pepper to the eggs.
  3. Whisk the ingredients until well combined.
  4. Heat butter in a pan over medium heat.
  5. Pour the egg mixture into the pan.
  6. Stir gently until the eggs are cooked to your liking.
  7. Remove from heat and serve immediately.
